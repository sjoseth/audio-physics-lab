1. The Tool (Simulation vs. Reality)

Audio Physics Lab is a theoretical simulation engine. It assumes ideal rectangular rooms with uniform construction materials.

Important Disclaimer: Real-world acoustics are complex. Furniture, windows, drywall flex, and architectural irregularities will affect actual sound. The results from this tool should be treated as a guide and a starting point for physical measurements, not absolute truth.
