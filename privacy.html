Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025 (Beta v2.0)

1. Zero-Data Collection

Audio Physics Lab is built with a "Privacy First" architecture. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data or input data.

All acoustic calculations (Room Modes, Ray Tracing, PEQ generation) are performed locally in your web browser using JavaScript. Your room dimensions and speaker positions never leave your device.

2. Local Storage

To improve your user experience, we use your browser's built-in Local Storage API. This allows the application to remember your last used room settings between visits.

  • This data is stored solely on your device.
  • You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache.

3. Analytics & Tracking

This application does not utilize third-party tracking pixels, cookies, or analytics services (like Google Analytics) within the application logic.
