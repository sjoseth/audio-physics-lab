Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-01-01

1. Overview

Your privacy is important to us. It is Audio Tools Suite's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or inputs you enter into our calculators. All calculations are performed locally in your web browser (client-side processing). Your room dimensions, speaker positions, and other settings never leave your device.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. We may use your browser's "Local Storage" to save your preferences (such as room dimensions) purely for your convenience, so you don't have to re-enter them every time you visit. This data remains on your device and is not accessible by us.

4. Third-Party Services

We use reputable third-party services for hosting and styling (such as Netlify and Tailwind CSS via CDN). These services may collect basic access logs (like IP addresses) for security and performance monitoring, which is standard practice for all websites.
